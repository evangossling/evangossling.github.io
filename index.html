About Me

I am a National Science Foundation (NSF) Graduate Research Fellow and Ph.D. student at Iowa State University (ISU) in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering (ECpE) under the mentorship of Professor Hongwei Zhang. I am affiliated with the Center for Wireless, Communities, and Innovation (WiCI) which involves the ARA Wireless Living Lab, part of the NSF Platforms for Advanced Wireless Research (PAWR) program.


My current research focus is at the transport layer, I am researching transport solutions to enable data intensive applications in highly dynamic and lossy environments. I am investigating multipath QUIC-based solutions, as well as multipath UDP with RaptorQ based encoding to increase reliability for real-time applications.
